# Sample UI Copy — Onboarding & Reward Flows

Voice: warm, plain-language, encouraging. Written for busy shop owners and their customers.

## Merchant onboarding
- **Landing hero:** "Turn one-time visitors into regulars." / "Replace paper stamp cards with a QR-first digital loyalty program."
- **CTA:** "Start 14-day free trial" · "No card required • Set up in under 5 minutes"
- **Wizard step 1:** "Tell us about your business" → "Step 1 of 2 · 14-day free trial, no card needed."
- **Wizard step 2:** "Create your account"
- **First campaign hint (templates):** "Buy 8 get 1 free", "First-visit welcome"
- **Empty campaigns:** "No campaigns yet — create your first loyalty program to start collecting stamps."
- **Empty QR:** "Create a QR and print it for your counter."

## Customer sign-in
- **Heading:** "Sign in to your rewards" / on scan: "Earn a stamp at {Brand}"
- **Sub:** "No app needed — sign in with your mobile number."
- **OTP:** "Enter OTP" · hint "Sent to {phone}"

## Scan & stamp
- **Store header:** "You're collecting stamps at {Brand}" · "📍 {Branch}"
- **CTA:** "⭐ Collect my stamp"
- **Consent line:** "By collecting, you agree to receive loyalty updates. You can opt out anytime."
- **Success (stamp):** "Stamp added!" · "{N} more → {Reward}"
- **Success (reward):** "🎉 Reward unlocked!" · "You've earned {Reward}" · CTA "View my reward"
- **Pending (staff approval):** "Waiting for staff — show this screen to a staff member to confirm your stamp."

## Fraud messages (friendly, non-accusatory)
- Cooldown: "Please wait {N} more minute(s) before your next stamp."
- Daily limit: "You've reached today's stamp limit for this reward."
- Geo missing: "Location is required to earn a stamp here. Please enable location and try again."
- Geo out of range: "You appear to be too far from the store to earn a stamp."
- Velocity: "Unusual activity detected on this device. Please try again later."

## Reward wallet & redemption
- **Unlocked:** "Show this code to staff to redeem" · expiry "Expires {date}"
- **Claimed:** "✓ Claimed {date}"
- **Staff verify:** "Verify a reward" → on claim "✓ Reward claimed!" · double-claim "This reward has already been claimed."

## Re-engagement templates
- "Hi {name}! You're just 1 stamp away from a free coffee at {brand} ☕"
- "Your reward at {brand} expires in 3 days. Visit us soon!"
- "We miss you at {brand}! Here's a bonus stamp on your next visit."
- "Happy birthday {name}! 🎂 Enjoy a free treat this week at {brand}."
- "Loved your reward? Leave us a Google review 🙏 {reviewLink}"
